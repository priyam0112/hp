Kerala

Kerala, known as 'God's Own Country,' offers stunning backwaters and lush greenery.
Explore the rich cultural heritage in cities like Kochi and Thiruvananthapuram.
Enjoy a peaceful stay in traditional houseboats.
The beautiful Western Ghats offer breathtaking views and rich biodiversity.
Don't miss the vibrant festivals that showcase the local culture.

Kashmir

Kashmir, the 'Paradise on Earth,' is famous for its Dal Lake and mesmerizing landscapes.
Experience the beauty of the Himalayas with serene snow-capped peaks.
Take a Shikara ride for a unique experience.
Visit the enchanting Gulmarg, known for its skiing resorts and meadows.
Discover the rich history and culture at the Mughal Gardens.
